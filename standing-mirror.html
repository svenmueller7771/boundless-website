Standing mirror

Elegant & timeless

Our Standing mirror graces any room with spacious impressions and are perfectly suited for those daily inspo outfits.

Available in four varieties: blonde-brown Oak, white Ash, red-brown Kiaat & brown Walnut.

The Standing mirror is purposefully crafted out of a single piece of solid wood, which means the the grain of the wood follows right through, giving the mirror a more undivided and natural look.

Dimensions

The Standing mirror has a height of 1750mm and is best positioned a few centimeters away from the wall. This allows the mirror frame to rest against the wall while the mirror itself remains upright, thanks to skillfully crafted angled joinery.
