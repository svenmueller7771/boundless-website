Introducing, the Akupanel. Timeless by Nature.

A modern, aesthetic wood slatted acoustic panel made from recycled plastics and solid wood.

Easy to install, built to last, and make styling any space, simple.

Available in Oak or white Ash wood, and black felt.

Wood types

Oak

Classic choice. Colours can range from light tan to medium brown with distinctive grain patterns. Timeless appearance.

Brown Oak

Oiled and stained by hand with 20% brown mineral wax oil to give a slightly darker shade of brown ~ medium brown.

Smoked Oak

Treated with smoke to achieve a darker, richer colour and a more distinctive grain pattern.

White Ash

Popular choice. Creamy white and light in colour. Contemporary appearance.

Black Ash

Oiled and stained by hand with a 80% black mineral wax oil.

Walnut

A rich, warm, deep chocolate brown. Luxurious appearance.

Installation

If you intend on using our panels for insulation or decorative purposes, we suggest affixing them directly to the wall or ceiling by screwing them through the acoustic felt, using the black screws that come included with your order. Alternatively, you can attach the panels by gluing them directly to the surface. Additionally, we offer an on-site service fee to install the panels for you (Western Cape only).

Our panels are versatile and can be mounted on a range of surfaces, including brick, concrete, and plasterboard. Moreover, they can be easily adapted to fit around any object. Simply use a saw to cut the slats widthways and a sharp blade to trim the felt lengthways, and you'll be able to seamlessly apply the panels around any item on your surface.

Conscious craftsmanship

Our commitment to environmental conservation is at the core of everything we do.

That's why we take pride in offering acoustic felt boards made from recycled plastic, and source all of our wood from sustainable suppliers.

We recognize the importance of preserving our forests, and as part of our efforts to give back, we have partnered with the Greenpop Foundation and joined their tree-volution movement! By doing so, we aim to ensure that for every tree we use, many more are planted to help sustain our natural resources for generations to come. For every items unit you purchase, we donate R50.

Delivery of goods

Our akupanels are delivered in a cardboard box & paper mesh (an eco-friendly alternative to bubble wrap) to protect the condition & integrity of your product during transport & to minimize our carbon footprint.
