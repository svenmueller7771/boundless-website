Frame Gallery

A perfect excuse to find those favourites, asap.

Turn your favourite artworks, photos, polaroids, or pressed flowers into a gallery.

Why 'floating'? By design, the picture frames don't have a backing board, and instead have two panels of glass. This allows the artwork to "float" with a border that inherits the colour of your wall, and allows the frame to blend in with its environment.

Don't have art? That's okay! We've curated some 4-piece & 8-piece galleries for you that are fitted with wholesome artworks from local artists, like FranklySaid & Stephané Van Eck fine art prints.

4-piece frame gallery

The 4-piece floating frame gallery includes 2 x A2 and 2 x A3 floating frames, with or without art.

Depending on your preferred spacing in between the frames you can expect a total layout of approximately 940 x 1240mm.



8-piece frame gallery

The 8-piece floating frame gallery includes 2 x A1, 4 x A3 and 2 x A4 floating frames, with or without art.

Depending on your preferred spacing in between the frames you can expect a total layout of approximately 2030 x 1360mm.

Installation

Our floating frames can be either wall-mounted or placed securely on the floor or a table leant against a wall.

If you intend on hanging the floating frames yourself, we suggest affixing them using the D-rings attached to the frame. The D-rings hook onto screws in the wall, one on each side of the frame. No string is used, to ensure the floating effect is preserved.

Additionally, we offer an on-site service fee to install the floating frames for you (Western Cape only).

Conscious craftsmanship

Our commitment to environmental conservation is at the core of everything we do.

That's why we take pride in offering acoustic felt boards made from recycled plastic, and source all of our wood from sustainable suppliers.

We recognize the importance of preserving our forests, and as part of our efforts to give back, we have partnered with the Greenpop Foundation and joined their tree-volution movement! By doing so, we aim to ensure that for every tree we use, many more are planted to help sustain our natural resources for generations to come. For every items unit you purchase, we donate R50.



Delivery of goods

Please note that the frame galleries can be ordered with or without the art displayed, if you choose to select the frames-only option we will include everything you’ll need to insert your own art and hang up the frames (excl. tools)

The product is delivered in a cardboard box & paper mesh (an eco-friendly alternative to bubble wrap) to protect the condition & integrity of your product during transport & to minimize our carbon footprint.
