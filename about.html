How it started

This story goes back to primary school and our favourite subject: woodwork.

While we had not developed the acumen for entrepreneurship and business - one thing was always certain - the aptitude we had to create expressive goods from raw materials.

Who we are

The Boundless Collective, aka boundless, was founded in 2022 by two best friends with a common passion for design, entrepreneurship and community.
