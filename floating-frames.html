Floating frames

Frame your art, darling.

Each floating frame is intentionally crafted from a single piece of solid wood, ensuring the natural grain flows seamlessly through the frame; providing a more natural appearance.

Why 'floating'? By design, the picture frames don't have a backing board, and instead have two panels of glass. This allows the artwork to "float" with a border that inherits the colour of your wall, and allows the frame to blend in with its environment.

Available in three varieties: brown-blonde Oak, white Ash & red-brown Kiaat.

Size Guide

If you would like to print or fit your own art we recommend downsizing by one size (or smaller) than the size of the floating frame. For example, if you are getting an A2 floating frame, then our size guide recommends A3 (or smaller) artworks and prints. This ensures the artwork still has an equal border, creating the floating effect.

We offer the following floating frame sizes:

A0: 1188 x 841mm

A1: 841 x 594mm

A2: 594 x 420mm

A3: 420 x 297mm

A4: 297 x 210mm

The A0 is our XL floating frame and is ideal for artworks and prints size A0 for borderless, or size A1 for borders and the floating effect.

The A4 is our XS floating frame and is ideal for size A4 for borderless, or size A5 for the floating effect.

Installation

Our floating frames are versatile enough to be displayed as a stunning wall-mounted art pieces, or placed securely on the floor or a table, effortlessly enhancing the ambiance of your living space.

If you intend on hanging the floating frames yourself, we suggest affixing the to the wall using the D-rings attached to the frame. The D-rings hook onto screws in the wall, one on each side of the frame. No string is used, to ensure the floating effect is preserved.

Additionally, we offer an on-site service fee to install the floating frames for you (Western Cape only).

Conscious craftsmanship

Our commitment to environmental conservation is at the core of everything we do.

That's why we take pride in offering acoustic felt boards made from recycled plastic, and source all of our wood from sustainable suppliers.

We recognize the importance of preserving our forests, and as part of our efforts to give back, we have partnered with the Greenpop Foundation and joined their tree-volution movement! By doing so, we aim to ensure that for every tree we use, many more are planted to help sustain our natural resources for generations to come. For every items unit you purchase, we donate R50.

What's included in my order?

By default, your order do not include the artwork prints displayed in the floating frames. These are sold separately on our site, or as part of our frame galleries.

The floating frames come with D-rings attached to hang the floating frame onto a wall. Rubber glue dots are included to keep your art in place between the two mineral glass sheets, once fitted.

The product is delivered in a cardboard box & paper mesh (an eco-friendly alternative to bubble wrap) to protect the condition & integrity of your product during transport & to minimize our carbon footprint.
